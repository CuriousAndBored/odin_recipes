A Recipe For Fried Rice

Description

A simple recipe to follow when you crave fried rice. This delicious meal is often served in chinese restaurants, often praised for how fast, easy and tantalizing it is!

    Ingredients:
  • 8 large prawns shelled & deveined ($2.50)
  • 1.50 cups short-grain rice overnight ($0.258)
  • 2 large eggs $0.40
  • 3 stalks spring onions chopped ($0.30)
  • 1.50 tsp chicken powder $0.098
  • 1 tsp light sauce $0.028
  • 3.50 tbsp oil $0.409
    Steps:
  1. Shell, then devein prawns using a satay stick.
  2. Place prawns in ice water prior to frying to achieve crunchy, juicy prawns. Pat dry before frying.
  3. In a hot wok, add 0.5 tbsp of oil and stir fry prawns till they turn orange. Remove from wok. Rinse wok if required.
  4. Add remaining 3 tbsp of oil into hot wok and add eggs. Scramble. Add overnight rice in and stir fry, pressing down on rice with the back of the spatula to loosen any clumps.
  5. Add chicken powder, light sauce & spring onions and fry. Finally, return prawns to the wok and fry. Serve once done.
